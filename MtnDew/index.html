Mountain Dew Map

Lindsay McClintock

This Mapbox style is based on the marketing campaign of Mountain Dew.

The map makes use of the bold font of the logo for the labels.The thick border of the logo is seen in the linework of the map.

The dew drops on the bottle can be seen in the water bodies on the map.

Downtown Minneapolis, Minnesota shows the geometric designs used on Mountain Dew bottles and cans. It also shows a dense road network of thick red lines.

The mountains near Juneau, Alaska show the colors of Mountain Dew on real mountains.

The Amazon River Delta shows the bright green of the water with dew drops inspired by the marketing photo of the bottle.

The Sahara Desert in Libya shows a sparsely populated area with few roads or water.

A zoomed-out view of Tennessee, where Mountain Dew started. The checkered pattern pays tribute to the old marketing mascot Willy.

A mid-scale view of Knoxville, Tennessee. The cities are represented with pop tabs.

A large-scale view of downtown Knoxville.

Neyland Stadium and other buildings part of the University of Tennessee campus.
